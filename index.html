Judicial Branch Jeopardy

Review game on the U.S. Judicial Branch & Supreme Court. By Darius Zafar

How to Play

  • Click a dollar amount under a category to reveal a clue.
  • Type your answer in the box and press Submit Answer.
  • Correct answers add the clue value to your score; incorrect answers subtract it.
  • Use Show Answer if you are stuck (no score change).
  • When all tiles are used, press Reset Game to play again.

Designed for civics review. Content focuses on the U.S. judicial branch, federal courts, and landmark Supreme Court cases.
